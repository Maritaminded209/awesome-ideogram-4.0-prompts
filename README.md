## 📖 Best practices for prompt engineering

The quality of your image depends on the details in your prompt.

* **Be specific:** Instead of writing "a cat," write "an orange tabby cat sitting on a blue velvet sofa."
* **Mention light:** Describe the lighting, such as "golden hour sunshine" or "soft studio lighting."
* **State the style:** Include terms like "cinematic," "minimalist," or "oil painting" to define the look.
* **Use references:** Mention camera types or film stock if you want a specific photographic look.
